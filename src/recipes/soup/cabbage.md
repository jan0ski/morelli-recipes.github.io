# Savoy Cabbage
*(Serves 6-8)*

* 1 large head of Savoy cabbage
* Water for steaming
* ¾ cup oil
* 2 - 3 cloves garlic
* Salt, pepper

1. Clean, section, and steam cabbage until tender, but firm
2. Drain water, leaving a ½ cup
3. Add oil and spices
4. Stir through cabbage
5. Continue to steam until tender
6. Serve
