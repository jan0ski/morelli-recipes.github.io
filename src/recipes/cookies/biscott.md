# Biscott
* 6 eggs
* 1 ½ cups sugar
* 1 ½ cups oil
* 1 tbs vanilla
* 1 tsp salt
* 3½ cups flour
* 1 tbs baking powder
* 1 tbs Anise Seeds

1. Mix well
2. Grease 9 x 13 cookie sheet
3. Bake 25 minutes at 375°F
4. Cool slightly
5. Slice
6. Place cut side down on a cookie sheet
7. Put in 375°F.oven until roasted
